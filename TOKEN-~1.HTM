Reading this honestly

"Don't ignore low-hanging fruit" deviates most (72.4% vs 87.8%): both runs use tiny artifacts there, so a few tokens swing the ratio — the row is noisy by nature and was flagged as an estimate in the original. And the biggest-lever rank swapped: the replication's log was larger, so "pull deltas" (508,740) overtook "trim output" (309,540). That is the point, not a problem — which lever is biggest depends on your session's shape; that the levers are 87–100% cuts does not. Moves 5, 6, and 10 remain constructed estimates in both runs (a wrong build you never make can't be fully measured). The replication total being ~2.1x the published total says the artifacts were bigger, nothing more — on a subscription plan both numbers are headroom against usage limits, not cash.
